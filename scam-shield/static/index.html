Quick rules that beat most scams

  • If the discount looks impossible (70–90% off brand goods), it is.
  • Never pay by bank transfer, gift card or crypto — you can't get that money back. Use a card or PayPal.
  • Search the shop's name plus the word “scam” before your first order.
  • Countdown timers and “only 2 left!” are pressure tricks — real stores restock.
  • A real shop shows a physical address, a working phone/email and a returns policy.
